Foxkit Special Fried Rice Recipe


Foxkit's Special Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 6 cloves of galric
  • 1 inch of ginger
  • ½ tablespoon of seasame oil
  • 1/8 ground pork or beef
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 stick of carrots
  • 2 pieces of eggs
  • 1/4 chopped green onions
  • 3 cups of cooked day old rice

Directions
Step 1
Make the sauce to incorporate to the fried rice. Grate three cloves of garlic and the ginger into a small bowl and then add in the sugar, soy sauce, seasame oil and vinegar.

Step 2
Sauté the garlic until it's brown and the union until translucent in a wok or large pan.

Step 3
Add in the ground pork or beef and brown the pork until there's no more visible pink color on the meat.

Step 4
Add in the carrots and mix in 2 tablespoon of the sauce for the fried rice to give flavor to the meat.

Step 5
Move the browned meat to one side of the pan or wok and put in your eggs. Once the egg is fully cooked, mix it within the meat and carrots until eveything is well incorporated.

Step 6
Add in the carrots and mix in 2 tablespoon of the sauce for the fried rice to give flavor to the meat.

Step 7
Add in the rice and incorporate the sauce slowly as to not make the rice soggy and wet too quick while cooking the fried rice.

Step 8
Finish the dish by adding pepper or MSG to your liking and add in the green onions. Enjoy1
